Charts & Formulas

Weight & Balance

Single Item (WAM Formula):

Moment = Weight × Arm
Weight = Moment ÷ Arm
Arm = Moment ÷ Weight

Aircraft Total CG:

Total CG = Sum of Moments ÷ Sum of Weights

Ballast Shift Weight:

Ballast = (Aircraft Wt × (Target CG - Current CG)) ÷ (Ballast Arm - Target CG)

Equipment Change (Alteration)

Step 1: Find New Weight

New Wt = Old Wt - Removed Wt + Installed Wt

Step 2: Find New Moment

New Moment = Old Moment - Removed Moment + Installed Moment

Step 3: Calculate New CG

New CG = New Moment ÷ New Wt

Sheet Metal

Rivet Diameter:

Diameter = 3 × Thickest Sheet Layer

Rivet Length:

Length = Total Thickness + (1.5 × Rivet Diameter)

Edge Distance (ED):

Universal (2D) = 2 × Rivet Diameter
Flush (2.5D) = 2.5 × Rivet Diameter

Rivet Pitch (Spacing):

Pitch = (Length - (2 × ED)) ÷ (Rivet Count - 1)

Bend Allowance (BA):

BA = ((0.01743 × R) + (0.0078 × T)) × Angle

Setback (SB) & Sightline:

SB = K-Factor × (Radius + Thickness)
Sightline = Flat Dimension + Radius

Electrical

Ohm's & Watt's Law Wheel:

Volts (E): I × R | P ÷ I | √(P × R)
Amps (I): E ÷ R | P ÷ E | √(P ÷ R)
Ohms (R): E ÷ I | E² ÷ P | P ÷ I²
Watts (P): E × I | I² × R | E² ÷ R

Motor Efficiency (Watts):

Watts = (Horsepower × 746) ÷ Efficiency Decimal

General Math

Proportions (Cross-Multiplication):

If A ÷ B = C ÷ D:
A = (B × C) ÷ D
B = (A × D) ÷ C

Percentages:

% of: (X ÷ 100) × Y
is what %: (X ÷ Y) × 100
% change: ((New - Old) ÷ Old) × 100

Aircraft Hardware

Physics

Pascal's Law (Force/Pressure/Area):

Force = Pressure × Area

Mechanical Work:

Work = Force × Distance

Mechanical Advantage:

Effort × Length = Resistance × Height

Hydraulic Displacement (Conservation of Volume):

Area₁ × Distance₁ = Area₂ × Distance₂

Double Acting Cylinder:

Force = Pressure × (Piston Area - Rod Area)

Brake Mean Effective Pressure (BMEP):

BMEP = (BHP × 792,000) ÷ (RPM × Displacement)

Temperature Conversions

Fahrenheit ↔ Celsius:

°C = (°F - 32) × 5/9
°F = (°C × 9/5) + 32

Celsius to Kelvin:

K = °C + 273.15

Fahrenheit to Rankine:

°R = °F + 459.67

Tool Drawer Formulas

Torque Wrench Extension:

Indicated Torque = (Target Torque × Wrench Length) ÷ (Wrench Length + Extension Length)

Torque Conversions:

in-lb = ft-lb × 12
ft-lb = in-lb ÷ 12

Metric ↔ Imperial:

mm = inches × 25.4
inches = mm ÷ 25.4
